This project is licensed under the **GNU AGPLv3** (see [LICENSE](LICENSE)).

By contributing, you agree to the **Contributor License Agreement** in
[CLA.md](CLA.md). You keep ownership of your work and grant the project the right
to relicense it (which keeps a future commercial / dual-license offering
possible).

**You accept the CLA by signing off your commits.** Add a `Signed-off-by` line to
every commit — `git commit -s` does this automatically:

```
Signed-off-by: Your Name <your.email@example.com>
```

This sign-off certifies the
[Developer Certificate of Origin](https://developercertificate.org/) and your
acceptance of [CLA.md](CLA.md).
